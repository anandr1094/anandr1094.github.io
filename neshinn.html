Samarpan Nesh Inn

About

SAMARPAN NESH INN is located in Patna. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free W WiFi throughout the property. The accommodation provides a shared lounge, an ATM and currency exchange for guests. All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a wardrobe. At the hotel, every room is equipped with a seating area. Guests at PATLIPUTRA SAMARPAN NESH INN can enjoy a continental breakfast. The nearest airport is Jay Prakash Narayan, 7 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
